Company History

Vasu Medical Agency was founded in 2014 with the goal of creating a reliable pharmaceutical distribution network. The company started as a small medicine supply business serving local pharmacies.

Through dedication, strong partnerships with pharmaceutical companies, and reliable service, the company expanded its product range and distribution network.

Today the company supplies medicines to healthcare providers while maintaining high standards of quality, storage, and distribution practices.
